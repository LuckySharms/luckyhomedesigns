Who We Are

Lucky Home Designs ("we," "us," "our") is a home organization content brand that publishes curated product recommendations and visual inspiration on Pinterest and our website at luckyhomedesigns.com. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our website and our use of third-party services including the Pinterest API.

Information We Collect

Website visitors: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services on this website. We do not collect personal information from visitors unless you voluntarily contact us by email.

Pinterest account data: Our application connects to Pinterest through their official API (v5) using OAuth 2.0 authorization. When authorized, we access our own Pinterest account data including board information, pin content, and performance analytics. We do not access, collect, or store data from any other Pinterest user's account.

Email correspondence: If you contact us at hello@luckyhomedesigns.com, we retain your message and email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Pinterest Data

We use the Pinterest API exclusively to manage our own content — this includes creating and publishing pins to our boards, organizing board structures, and reviewing performance analytics to understand which content resonates with our audience. All API activity is limited to our own Pinterest business account.

We do not use Pinterest data to build user profiles, target advertising, sell or share data with third parties, or collect information about Pinterest users who interact with our pins.

Data Storage and Security

Pinterest OAuth tokens are stored securely on a private server infrastructure with restricted access. Access tokens are short-lived (30-day expiry) and refresh tokens are rotated according to Pinterest's recommended security practices. We do not store Pinterest user data beyond what is necessary to publish and manage our own content.

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages with HTTPS encryption enabled for all connections.

Third-Party Services

Our content may include affiliate links to products on Amazon and other retailers. When you click an affiliate link from one of our pins, you leave our content and are subject to that retailer's own privacy policy. We receive referral commissions through the Amazon Associates program but do not receive personally identifiable information about purchasers.

We use DigitalOcean for server infrastructure and image hosting. Their privacy policy governs data processed on their platform.

Data Retention

We retain Pinterest API tokens only for as long as needed to manage our content publishing operations. Tokens are revoked and deleted if our application is deauthorized or if we cease using the Pinterest API. Email correspondence is retained for up to 12 months unless you request earlier deletion.

Your Rights

If you believe we hold any personal information about you, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of that information by contacting us. Pinterest users can revoke our application's access at any time through their Pinterest account settings, which immediately terminates our ability to access any account data.

Children's Privacy

Our website and content are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of our website or content after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@luckyhomedesigns.com.
