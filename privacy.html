Who We Are

Lucky Home Designs ("we," "us," "our") is a home organization content brand. We publish curated product recommendations and visual inspiration on social media and on our website at luckyhomedesigns.com. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our website and related services.

Information We Collect

Website visitors: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services on this website. We do not collect personal information from visitors unless you voluntarily contact us by email.

Email correspondence: If you contact us at contact@luckyhomedesigns.com, we retain your message and email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

Social Media and Third-Party Platforms

We use third-party platforms and their official APIs to publish and manage our content. This includes social media services such as Pinterest, where we connect via OAuth 2.0 authorization to manage our own account — creating posts, organizing boards, and reviewing performance analytics.

All platform API activity is limited to managing our own brand accounts. We do not access, collect, or store data from other users' accounts on any platform. We do not use platform data to build user profiles, target advertising, or sell data to third parties.

Affiliate Links

Our content may include affiliate links to products on Amazon, Target, Wayfair, and other retailers. When you click an affiliate link, you leave our content and are subject to that retailer's own privacy policy. We receive referral commissions through programs such as Amazon Associates but do not receive personally identifiable information about purchasers.

Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens for third-party services are stored securely on private server infrastructure with restricted access. Tokens are short-lived and rotated according to each platform's recommended security practices. We do not store user data beyond what is necessary to publish and manage our own content.

Data Retention

We retain platform authentication tokens only for as long as needed to manage our content operations. Tokens are revoked and deleted if access is deauthorized or if we cease using a platform's services. Email correspondence is retained for up to 12 months unless you request earlier deletion.

Your Rights

If you believe we hold any personal information about you, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of that information by contacting us. Users of third-party platforms can revoke our application's access at any time through their account settings, which immediately terminates our ability to access any account data.

Children's Privacy

Our website and content are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at contact@luckyhomedesigns.com.
